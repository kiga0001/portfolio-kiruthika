About Me

I am from India and have been residing in Sweden for almost two years with my husband. Before moving to Sweden, I worked as a kindergarten teacher and hold a master's degree in Mathematics.

I have two siblings, and one of my fondest childhood memories is making small objects from the soil in the garden with them. One day, I created a small pot and showed it to my father. He appreciated my work, and I was very happy that day.
